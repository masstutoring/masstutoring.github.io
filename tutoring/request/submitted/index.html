Request received

Thanks — we've got your request.

You don't need to do anything else right now. Here's exactly what happens next.

Your reference: the email address you entered is your reference — mention it if you contact us. If you'd like a copy of your request, the form can email you your responses.

What happens next

  1. We acknowledge your request

    You'll get a confirmation email. If it doesn't arrive, check spam, then email masstutoringea@gmail.com.

  2. We look for a match

    We aim to match you with a tutor within about a week, depending on volunteer availability in your subject and time zone. You'll hear from us by email either way.

  3. If we're at capacity

    We'll tell you honestly and keep your request active, and point you to a free group session or the Guide in the meantime — we won't leave you without a response.

Need to change or withdraw your request?

Email masstutoringea@gmail.com to update your availability, correct a detail, or withdraw and delete your request entirely. No reason needed, and no questions asked.

What to do while you wait

Your first session is far more useful if your tutor can see real practice results. A great head start:

  • Work through the Start Here guide.
  • Take a full practice test in Bluebook and review every miss with the diagnose-your-mistakes guide.
  • Prefer live group help now? Schoolhouse.world's free SAT bootcamps are a strong complement while you wait.
