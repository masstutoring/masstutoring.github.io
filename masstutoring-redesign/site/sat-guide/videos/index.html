Videos and creators

YouTube has excellent free SAT instruction — buried in an ocean of clickbait. Everything below is individually reviewed: we link the exact videos worth your time, and say so when a creator also sells something.

How to use video without wasting time: watch to learn a specific skill you've identified from a practice test, then immediately drill that skill with real questions. An hour of watching with no practice moves your score approximately zero points.

How we choose (and keep) creators and videos

  • Content must target the current digital SAT, not the retired paper format.
  • Individual videos are published only after we verify the URL, title, length, and caption availability.
  • Free videos must be genuinely useful on their own — not just ads for a paid program. We disclose every paid product a creator promotes, and we never take payment or affiliate commissions for a listing.
  • We re-review listings periodically and remove any that go stale or paywall their useful content. Each card shows its verification date.
