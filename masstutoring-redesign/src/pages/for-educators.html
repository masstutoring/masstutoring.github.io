For counselors, teachers & librarians

One free link for the students who need it most.

Mass Tutoring is a free, student-run SAT resource. There are two things you can point a student to, both at no cost and with no account required:

  • The SAT Guide — the best free, verified SAT resources (official College Board tools, videos, books, study plans), organized so a student always knows the next step. See the guide.
  • Free tutoring — one-on-one help from selected, trained student tutors (chosen for teaching ability, with a recent 1530+ digital-SAT score as one factor). No cost, and we never ask about a family's finances. How tutoring works.

Nothing here costs money, asks for payment information, or requires an account. If a student only has a phone, the whole site works on it.

Ways to work with us

  • Refer one student — use the form below; we'll follow up with the right next step.
  • Request an information session or SAT workshop — for a class, club, or group of students.
  • Host a group pilot — the proposed SAT Access Corps is a free six-week program we'd like to bring to a first partner site. It's not running yet — we're looking for a pilot partner.
  • Discuss a partnership — ongoing collaboration between your school or library and Mass Tutoring.

What we don't promise: we won't claim your school endorses us without written approval, and we won't overstate capacity — we're a student-run program and we'll be honest about what we can take on.
