Official College Board Completely Free

The official library of real SAT Suite questions. You can filter by section (Math or Reading and Writing), by content domain, by specific skill, and by difficulty, then export a custom practice set with answer explanations.

CB

Bluebook (Official Digital Testing App) (opens in a new tab)

Bluebook is the app you will use on real test day. It includes full-length official adaptive practice tests, test previews, and the same built-in Desmos calculator, timer, annotation, and flagging tools you'll have on the actual SAT.

Official College Board Completely Free
KA

Khan Academy Official Digital SAT Prep (opens in a new tab)

Free structured lessons, video instruction, and practice exercises for both SAT sections, built with College Board as the official practice partner. After a Bluebook practice test, you can link your results to get personalized practice recommendations.

Official Practice Partner Completely Free
CB

Official Full-Length SAT Practice Tests (opens in a new tab)

College Board's hub for full-length practice: adaptive digital tests in Bluebook plus downloadable linear (nonadaptive) practice tests for students who need paper or accommodated formats.

Official College Board Completely Free
CB

SAT Registration (opens in a new tab)

The official place to register for the SAT, view upcoming test dates and deadlines, and manage an existing registration. Registration itself requires a College Board account; the test has a fee unless you qualify for a fee waiver.
